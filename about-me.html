Prior To Teaching

Since finishing school, i've done a number of things to keep busy. Initially, I studied criminology at the University of Wollongong. However this didn't last long, I withdrew from the course after only 6 months! I wanted to earn some money without having to work on the weekends and uni made that difficult, so I started working as an electrical apprentice. This happened to be one of the best moves I could have made! I loved working in the trade and started off working residential before moving to commercial. I enjoy working with my hands and was glad to be earning some money!

I had some great teachers during my apprenticeship, not only at work but also at TAFE. People who were passionate about what the were teaching stood out as people I aspired to be like. Eventually, I wanted to encourage school leavers to get into a trade, so I decided to go to uni and study teaching!
